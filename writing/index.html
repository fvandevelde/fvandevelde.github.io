Blog

Started in late 2019 just before the start of the pandemic. My blog features writing on art, technology, trans issues, and pedagogy. I started the blog as a way to move my writing off of social media platforms and into a space that I had more authorship over. The blog used to have a comments section but it was retired in the transfer from my old site to my new one. I would still love to here your thoughts though!
